Audi Quattro S1
The Ultimate Pikes Peak Champion

Audi Quattro S1 E2 gave Walter Röhrl the opportunity for his record breaking run of 10:47.850 and be the first ever competitor to reach the peak under 11 minutes at the legendary Pikes Peak “Race to the Clouds” event. He also defeated a fleet of three specially prepared 205 T16s that Peugeot had entered in hopes to stop Audi’s reign at the event. In the end, Audi had won its gamble and immediately retired the rally quattro from competition, but this time as a legendary champion. Audi’s motorsport aspirations thereafter shifted to circuit racing.
